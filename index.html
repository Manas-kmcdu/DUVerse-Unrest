How it works

Simple as semester 1

01
Sign up with your college email
Use your official DU college email to verify your identity. You get a verified badge for your college - SRCC, Miranda, Hindu, whoever. No outsiders allowed.
02
Build your campus profile
Set your college, year, department, and interests. Follow people from your college and across DU. Your profile is your DU identity - more real than LinkedIn.
03
Post, discover, and connect
Share campus moments, ask questions, post opportunities. See what's happening at every DU college in real time. Join sports events, running clubs, and turf games right from the app.
04
Shop the campus store
Buy your college merch - tees, hoodies, notebooks with your college name. Sell old books and gear to fellow students. Commerce that actually makes sense for campus life.
